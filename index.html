About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at Department of Computer Science, Texas A&M University. I work with Dr. Jeff Huang on Programming Language and Software Engineering. My research interests include stateless model checking, concurrency debugging, symbolic execution and testing. Before that, I got my bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science, Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
