I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate at Department of Computer Science, Texas A&M University. I work for Dr. Jeff Huang on Software Engineering and Programming Languages. Before that, I got my bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science, Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I am doing some excited dynamic programming verification technique. To be more specific, I am working on model checking and concentrate on the Maximal Causality Reduction. In addition, I am still interested in DPOR and SAT based model checking. I am trying to implement these algorithms and make comparisons between them. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in this area!
